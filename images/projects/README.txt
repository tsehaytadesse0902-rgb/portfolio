Project Images Instructions:

Place your project images in this folder to showcase your Excel and Data Entry work.

Required images:
1. excel-dashboard.jpg - Excel Data Analysis Dashboard screenshot
2. data-entry.jpg - Data Entry & Database Management example
3. excel-templates.jpg - Excel Template Creation samples
4. data-migration.jpg - Data Migration & Cleanup project example

Image Recommendations:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: 600x400px or larger (maintain 3:2 aspect ratio)
- Quality: High resolution screenshots or photos
- Content: Screenshots of Excel work, data entry examples, or relevant visuals

Tips for creating project images:
- Take screenshots of your Excel dashboards and templates
- Show before/after examples of data organization
- Display data visualization charts and graphs
- Capture clean, professional-looking spreadsheets

You can also use placeholder images or stock photos related to data entry and Excel work until you have actual project screenshots.
